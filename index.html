Set up your wallet to purchase $SPMSL

Before you can buy SPMSL, you'll need a compatible Ethereum wallet. Popular choices include MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet. Ensure you have some Ethereum (ETH) in your wallet to use for the transaction.

2

Connect your wallet to Uniswap

  • Go to the Uniswap website (https://app.uniswap.org/).
  • Click on "Use Uniswap" in the top right corner.
  • A pop-up will appear asking you to connect your wallet. Select your wallet of choice.
3

Set up your wallet to purchase $SPMSL

  • In Uniswap, click on the "Select a token" field and paste the SPMSL contract address:
    0xd3140d263eA08768B31712434261B71c4881f2bc.
  • SPMSL should now appear. Click on it to select.
4

Swap ETH for $SPMSL

  • Enter the amount of ETH you want to exchange for SPMSL in the "Input" field. The equivalent amount of SPMSL will automatically calculate based on the current exchange rate.
  • Review the transaction details, including estimated gas fees.
  • Click "Swap" and confirm the transaction in your wallet when prompted.
  • Once confirmed, the SPMSL tokens will be transferred to your wallet.
  • And there you have it! You've successfully purchased SPMSL on Uniswap.
